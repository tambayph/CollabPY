Isohyetal Map

An isohyetal map is a visual tool that shows how much it rains in different places during the day. It helps us understand the pattern and amount of rainfall in a specific area over a 24-hour period. By looking at this map, we can see when and where it rains the most, giving us useful information about the daily weather patterns. This type of map is particularly handy for meteorologists, farmers, and planners who need to know about rainfall distribution.
